Butter Chicken Curry

Description

Ingredients

  • 200g of chicken
  • Coriander powder
  • Cumin powder
  • Turmeric powder
  • 1/2 large onion
  • 50g yogurt
  • 50ml milk
  • Tomato puree
  • Olive oil
  • Thumb sized piece ginger and one clove of garlic, grated

Steps

  1. Heat the pan and add the oil. Wait until the oil is hot, then add the chicken.
  2. After the chicken browns on both sides, add the diced onion
  3. After sweating the onions for a few minutes, add the powdered spice
  4. Add the ginger and garlic and sautee for few minutes
  5. Add tomato puree and cook for 10 minutes
  6. Add yogurt and milk and cook for a further 15 miuntes
  7. Taste for salt and add as necessary

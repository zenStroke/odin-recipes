Tomato Eggs

Description

Ingredients

  • 2 large tomatoes
  • 3 eggs
  • salt
  • paprika
  • basil
  • cornstarch
  • 2 tbs water
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. Dice tomatoes into medium chunks and get rid of stems
  2. Heat oil in pan and cook the tomatoes for a few minutes until soft
  3. Add the mixture of cornstarch and water to the pan and mix well
  4. Remove the tomatoes from the pan. Add the three eggs into a small bowl and whisk. Add a tsp of salt
  5. Heat the pan again and add the eggs
  6. After half a minute, add the eggs and stir well
  7. Add the paprika, basil for garnish. Serve and eat while hot
